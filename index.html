// My Story

Games have been my thing for as long as I can remember. Growing up, titles like Halo: Combat Evolved and the classic LEGO games weren't just entertainment to me—they were these incredible worlds packed with art, sound, and clever design that I desperately wanted to understand. I'd spend hours picking apart every little detail, trying to figure out how it all worked. That curiosity? It never went away. Eventually, I realized I could turn that obsession into something more than just a hobby.

Before diving into world-building full-time, I spent a lot of time in the competitive gaming scene. I competed in British Esports Association tournaments at the T2 national level—playing across England, Scotland, Wales, and Ireland with the S6C team. Honestly, I wore a lot of hats there: head coach, analyst, and the team's second-best player (mostly running Chamber and Omen). I've poured thousands of hours into competitive gaming over the years—made it to Global Elite in CS2 and immortal 2 in Valorant, for what it's worth! //soul

// My Dream Project

Okay, so if you ask me about my dream project, I will absolutely talk your ear off about "Project Evergarden." It's a "Noble Dark" fantasy RPG that digs into themes of loss, loneliness, and finding meaning when everything feels broken. Picture this: medieval fantasy crashing headfirst into the eerie, dangerous ruins of a hyper-advanced civilization—all because of a cataclysm that literally fractured reality itself. Pretty wild, right?

What I really want is for players to feel something real when they play—the weight of tough choices, sure, but also those beautiful moments of genuine hope and hard-won victory. Your decisions will actually shape the world around you, its factions, and the people living in it. I've been working on a comprehensive GDD with a combat system featuring stance-switching mechanics, a magic system built around elemental combos and spell-crafting, and character progression driven by nine core stats.

And honestly? The sound design is where I get most excited. I want every single location to have its own personality—like the surreal Crystal Oasis Desert with its massive mana gems humming in the distance, or the haunting silence of the Dead Forest.

My goal is basically to blend FromSoftware's environmental storytelling and deliberate combat with BioWare's narrative depth and meaningful character relationships. Is it ambitious? Ridiculously so. Will it be worth every late night? Without a doubt.

// Boundaries & Terms

I really enjoy working with creative people on exciting projects—it's honestly one of the best parts of what I do! Before we dive in though, here are a few things that help set expectations so we can collaborate smoothly.

  • Content I Don't Work On: I respectfully pass on NSFW or pornographic projects. My focus is on creating engaging, respectful, and imaginative content that I'm proud to put my name on.
  • Payment Structure: For freelance work, I ask for 50% upfront before starting, with the remaining 50% due once I deliver the final assets. It's pretty standard in the industry and tends to work well for everyone involved!
  • Rates: Every project is different, so my pricing depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. Just shoot me the details about what you have in mind, and I'll put together a personalized quote—no pressure, no commitment.
  • Tools & Engines: For programming and development work, I specialize exclusively in Unity. That said, if you need art or SFX assets, I can absolutely create content that works with any engine, including Unreal 4 and 5.
