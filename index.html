// My Story

From a young age, I was captivated by the worlds inside my family's computer. Games like the original Halo: Combat Evolved and the classic LEGO titles weren't just entertainment; they were intricate, living systems of art, sound, and logic that I felt an overwhelming urge to understand and deconstruct. This childhood curiosity was the spark that drove me to turn a lifelong passion into a focused, professional craft, underpinned by a deep inquiry into the core principles of user experience and collaborative development.

Before dedicating myself to building worlds, I mastered them as a top-tier competitor. In tournaments hosted by the British Esports Association, I competed at a T2, national level across England, Scotland, Wales, and Ireland. Playing for the S6C team, I took on a hybrid role as head coach, analyst, and the team's second-best player, specializing in the Sentinel agent Chamber and the Controller Omen. I invested thousands of hours into the demanding ecosystem of esports, achieving the rank of Global Elite in CS2 and Ascendant 1 in Valorant. //soul

// My Dream Project

My ultimate goal is to bring "Project Evergarden" to life—a "Noble Dark" fantasy RPG that explores themes of loss, loneliness, and the search for meaning in a fractured world. It is a universe where medieval fantasy coexists with the haunting, often dangerous, remnants of a lost, hyper-advanced technological past, a world born from a cataclysm that shattered reality itself. The game will balance the grim realities of a fallen world with moments of genuine hope, defiant joy, and earned heroism. The design is built upon deep, systemic gameplay where player choices have a tangible and lasting impact on the world state, its factions, and its inhabitants. My GDD for the project outlines a complex combat system with stance-switching mechanics, a robust magic system with elemental synergies and dynamic spell-crafting, and a character progression defined by nine core stats. This will be combined with a rich, branching narrative and an unforgettable, atmospheric sound design that brings the world's unique biomes—from the surreal Crystal Oasis Desert with its giant mana gems to the charred, desolate Dead Forest of the World Tree—to vivid life. My vision is to blend the environmental storytelling, challenging deliberate combat, and intricate world design of a FromSoftware title with the narrative agency, deep character focus, and impactful choices of a classic BioWare RPG.

// Boundaries & Terms

I'm passionate about collaborating on exciting projects. To ensure a smooth and professional process, please review the following terms before getting in touch.

  • Project Scope: I do not accept work on projects that involve NSFW or pornographic content. My focus is on creating engaging, respectful, and creative experiences.
  • Payment: All freelance projects require a 50% upfront payment before work begins. The final 50% is due upon completion and delivery of the final assets.
  • Pricing: Rates vary depending on the scope, complexity, and timeline of the project. Please contact me with your project details for a custom quote.
  • Engine & Tools: For development and programming work, I operate exclusively within the Unity engine. For art and SFX assets, I can create content suitable for any engine, including Unreal Engine 4 and 5.
